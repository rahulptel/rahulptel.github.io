Rahul Patel

Hello! I am Rahul, a PhD student at the department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering, University of Toronto. I work with Prof. Elias Khalil on accelerating discrete optimization solution techniques using Machine Learning. More specifically, I work on Stochastic and Multi-objective optimization problems.

I completed master's in Industrial Engineering at Polytechnique Montreal, supervised by Andrea Lodi and Yoshua Bengio. I was at Shipmnts as a Machine Learning Engineer (2017-18) and Ahmedabad University as a Teaching Assistant (2016-17) before starting grad school.
