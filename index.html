About Me

I prefer to think of people as N dimensional vectors, where each dimension is the factor by which they are driven. And life is all about finding those vectors whose projection is maximum on yours.

I am excited about deep learning and its applications in natural language processing and computer vision. Currently -- apart from my full time work -- I work on Generative Adversarial Nets.

I hold a B.Tech. in Information and Communication Technology. As a lifelong learner, I love to study state-of-the-art techniques aimed at mitigating problems of humanity.

When technically at rest, I like to read non-fiction, specifically South-Asia geopolitics and swim.

ProjectsOther Projects

Generative Adversarial Nets (GANs)

GANs can be trained to learn the probability distribution from which the data samples have been generated. Once, trained they can produce new look-alike samples. We have experimented with GANs on image as well as text data. With images, we use ideas like Probabilistic Principle Component Analysis (Tipping et. al.), Factor Analysis and seed improvement to makes the Generator Network faster. Whereas with text, we try to generate english sentences and much more. Send an email to know more about the same.

Image reference: http://wiseodd.github.io/img/2016-09-17-gan-tensorflow/training.gif

CheckIt Go

[Playstore] | [Paper] | [Web]
CheckIt Go is a spinoff of an award winning research project. It has off lately turned into a handy tool for evaluating/checking OMR sheets used during exams using mobile phones. The application though working, is still in it's beta undergoing various crucial improvements.

visionEd

[Demo]
visionEd is a block based rapid prototyping typing tool for computer vision and image processing applications. On one hand, it is extremely easy for novice users to start experimenting with the blocks and visualize various algorithms on the other hand, the rich library helps the experts quickly testing out their ideas without much hassle.

Face detection on HD video

[Report]
Various algorithms like Local Binary Patterns(LBP), Constrained Local Models (CLM) regularized by Land-mark Mean Shift and Viola-Jones along with CAMSHIFT and KLT were implemented. The results of these algorithms were compared and considering the strengths and weaknesses of each of them a hybrid approach using Viola-Jones and CAMSHIFT was developed for real time face detection.
